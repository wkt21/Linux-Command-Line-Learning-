mv` — Move or Rename Files

mv moves files/directories or renames them.

---

Rename a file

mv oldname.txt newname.txt


---

Move a file to another directory

mv report.pdf /var/reports/


---

Move multiple files

mv *.log logs/


---

Interactive move (prompt before overwrite)

mv -i file.txt /tmp/


---

Prevent overwriting

mv -n file.txt /tmp/


---
